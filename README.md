## 📊 Understanding core parking

Processors divide work between different cores. When you do not use your computer, Windows turns off unused cores. This saves electricity. However, the move to wake those cores creates a lag. Gamers and content creators often notice this delay.

ParkControl-2026 prevents this sleep state. By stopping the parking process, you keep your system ready. You see immediate gains in responsiveness. This does not overclock your processor. It simply changes how Windows handles power management for your hardware.
